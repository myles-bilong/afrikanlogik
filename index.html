Welcome to Afrikan Logik

The Afro-centric movement

Afrikan Logik is a movement made up of people who want to tell the African story through African voices taking into cognissance, the African identity expressed through the culture and traditions in spheres like music, arts & crafts and drama.

AFRIKAN LOGIK SERVICES

Being Afro-centric, we strive to promote and project ourselves through our diverse culture, our music and our tradition amongst many.

MUSIC

Summit Records is a partner to Afrikan Logik. Here, musical talent is discovered, nurtured then exposed. Aspiring musical artists find Summit Records an ideal place where they can connect with other growing artists..

AUDIO-VISUALS

Film development and production that narrates our stories and history. In a world where little is credited to us, we make our own voices heard through our films.

MOTHER TONGUE

The era from post colonisation to independece saw a decline in our native tongue. Coming generations have severe difficulties expressing themselves in their native tongue. Afrikan Logik and her partners are working towards reawakening the love of our native languages.

AFRICAN LITERATURE

In order to preserve and narrate our rich history and heritage, we are nurturing aspiring writers and giving motivation & support to in-residence writers.

LEADERS,VISIONARISTS

Our greatest desire is to live in peace and unity with one another. We are pleased to have trained youths in leadership skills that will enable then effectively and efficiently address our problems and give us the Africa we Want.

ART

Every carving or artwork has a message behind it. Why dont you visit our shop to read these messages.
